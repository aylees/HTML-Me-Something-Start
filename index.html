Latest Reviews

Check Grow

Vibes Rating: 4/5

Coffee Rating: 1/5

Pros: A multi-story, reasonably priced cafe with food and drinks (the tea and cakes are wonderful). Check Grow is open late and close to a subway station, making it easy to visit. It's so easy to study or read here, and they do have 'quiet areas'. The views are great; you can see across the river.

Cons: The coffee is super watery, and the food is just okay, but a lot of people stop by the convenience store to grab some snacks. The weekends can get busy, especially if there is an event going on downtown.

Koriko

Vibes Rating: 4/5

Coffee Rating: 4/5

Pros: Koriko is a Studio Ghibli-themed cafe with a great seasonal selection of drinks and bakery items. All of the staff are very friendly. It's a perfect place to meet with friends.

Cons: The wait is almost always an hour plus, and you only have a limited time in the cafe. However, it's more of an experience and not a study or reading cafe.

Fall in Lemiu

Vibes Rating: 3/5

Coffee Rating: 5/5

Pros: The coffee is some of the best in Seoul, and the cafe is Instagrammable. Fall in Lemiu decorates according to the seasons and has a whole upstairs area with some interactive art pieces not pictured. They also have another floor dedicated to selling their makeup line, which is pretty unique.

Cons: The cafe is a bit far and a little difficult to get to. The seating is uncomfortable if staying for a long time, but there is a study cafe across the street.
